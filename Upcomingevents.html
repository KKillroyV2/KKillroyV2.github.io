Upcoming events

Newcastle Nights Upcoming Events, below are some of the best upcoming events in the Newcastle area ranging from beach parties to hot new DJs in town. Be sure to check this page often for information on must-see events.

Newcastle Beach Bomb Party

Newcastle might not be famous for its sunshine but our yearly beach parties make the most of what sunshine we get. A shuttle service is running between Newcastle City Centre and the Beach Event free of charge. Beach Bomb is on 23/4/2019 from 10:00 - 00:00 Tickets are £10 and come with one free drink.


One Night in Westlife at Powerhouse

Coming this July, the world-famous band Westlife will be performing at Newcastle’s Powerhouse, “One night with Westlife” will run for 120 minutes from 7pm - 9pm on July 13th 2019. Entrance to Powerhouse on the date of the event will start at £15


Newcastle Beer Boat cruise

Nothing says Newcastle more than a beer boat floating down the Tyne, hop aboard the USS Magpie for an evening of partying, drinking and cruising with your friends, co-workers or loved ones. These cruises start on May 1st and run each Friday and Saturday from 12:00 to 23:00. Tickets start from £20 and include two free drink vouchers.
