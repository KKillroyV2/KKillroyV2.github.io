Who are Newcastle Nights?

Established in 2005, Newcastle Nights is the premier social and event hub for all things Newcastle, whether you’re looking for pub crawls, popular bands and DJs or just a bite to eat in some of Newcastle's world-class restaurants, our site contains everything you could ever need. Newcastle Nights started the company in 2005 as a student nightlife organisation that have branched out since then creating a website that provides all of the need to know information for a good time in Newcastle, whether you’re a currently studying at a local establishment or are just looking for the place to be.
