Newcastle Nights
Local nightlife

Looking to grab a bite to eat before a big night out or wondering where the best drinks are served? Below we recommend our personal favourites whether it’s the best Kebab in town or a decent student bar.


Flares newcastle

With Flares bars retro vibe plus 70s-style wallpaper, booth seating and dance floor hung with glitter balls, it’s no wonder this is one of the best places for students to party and share drinks. Open 8 till late this bar is somewhere you don’t want to miss especially not with a shocking 5 stars on most review sites in newcastle..


Laus 202

With over 100 varieties to choose from, every visit will be a different experience. At our Chef’s Island we offer a generous variety of sushi, hot and cold starters, dim sum and teppanyaki and grill to order. Watch our professionally trained sushi chefs prepare food fresh while you wait for your teppanyaki and grill order to be cooked. Fresh waffles are also available. We are open daily from 11:30am till 10:30pm. .


Illegitimate Bar

The subterranean club called Illegitimate opened in September 2016 and adds to the upmarket regeneration of the classic Bigg Market.The venue is designed to be a more upmarket neighbour to Filthy's and Passing Cloud and has been named after a brand of cigarettes which were once made in Newcastle . Illegitimate is decked out with gold leather and velvet seats and delivers R&B nights until 3am..
