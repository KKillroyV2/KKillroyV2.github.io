Past events

Here are some of our most popular previous events, some of these events happen frequently so be sure to check out their pages and see if they’re your cup of tea.


Quayside Stall Market

The exciting Newcastle Quayside Market features independent traders selling handcrafted goods and local produce in an unrivaled location every Sunday from 9am until 8pm. Runs January to September


Digital refreshers opening party

At Digital club in newcastle, be sure not to miss this one week only Freshers themed event. Featuring some of the hottest local talent and special drinks to order. Entry costs just £5. Club opens each evening at 8pm and stays open past 2am


Ice skate celebrate

At the newly opened Newcastle Ice Rink located in the Centre for Life we’re celebrating this new addition to the town by lowering the entrance price to just £1 per hour and giving away free slushies for all customers. Get yourself down to the Centre for Life, Runs from 10 November 2018 - 24 February 2019 11am - 8pm
